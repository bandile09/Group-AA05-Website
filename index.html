So what is pervasive computing?

 

Pervasive computing is the idea of integrating computing capabilities and technology into commonplace things and situations such that they are smoothly incorporated into our daily lives is known as pervasive computing, also known as ubiquitous computing. Pervasive computing aims to provide people with access to computing resources and services whenever and wherever they need them, without requiring them to be aware of the underlying technology.
